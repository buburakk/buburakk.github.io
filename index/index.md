# Welcome!

I personally consider teaching and writing the two most effective ways&mdash;must-have skills&mdash;for learning anything. Writing to teach as a method is even more effective. It is not only a means of communication but also a tool that reinforces knowledge. Other than that, I just wanted to run a blog-like website. This was more or less the main motivation behind creating my personal blog and writing about a variety of topics such as engineering, mathematics, coding, and even Linux. In other words, here is a place&mdash;an archive of notes, code snippets, projects, etc.&mdash;where I share my personal experience with what I learn whether or not they are directly related to my field of expertise, for whoever happens to come by and might find it useful. Also please feel free to point out errors and suggest improvements to the content.
